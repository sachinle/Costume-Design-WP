SSCP

Semester Skill Certification Programme

Transforms every semester into a global credential milestone. Beyond the core curriculum, each student completes one internationally recognised certification per semester from leading global universities and corporate bodies, facilitated through reputed platforms such as Coursera, LinkedIn Learning, and other globally recognised learning networks.

Coursera LinkedIn Learning Adobe CC Google IBM Microsoft AWS Duke University Johns Hopkins NYU
SIIP

Semester Industry Immersion Programme

Every semester, students step into real industry environments through structured immersion experiences (2 to 10 days, designed as mini-internships). This exposure builds practical skills, industry confidence, and professional readiness well before graduation.

OLT

Outbound Leadership Training

Conducted annually once by corporate trainers, this programme builds leadership mindset, discipline, and confidence through experiential challenges and guided activities. Annual progress monitoring ensures continuous growth.

FEP

Field Exposure Programme

Organised annually once, offering structured visits to research centers, industries, and scientific institutions to connect classroom learning with real-world exposure. Travel, food & accommodation borne by students.

GIP

Global Immersion Programme

A structured international exposure initiative featuring:

  • Online certification training
  • Global collaborative project
  • Physical Global Immersion Visit

…to build global competence and perspective.
